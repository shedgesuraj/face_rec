WHAT THE WARNING MEANS
======================

You may see:
"Trying to unpickle estimator SVC from version 1.2.0 when using version 1.7.2"

This is NORMAL and harmless. It happens because:
- Model was trained with scikit-learn 1.2.0
- Your environment has scikit-learn 1.7.2
- System still works fine - just a compatibility notice

The model was already trained and works correctly.
